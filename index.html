        Tip box:

  • It is better to buy a caravan with a fixed bed, it is usually easier to sell them
  • To get a good caravan you need to invest 20K-25K NZD, do not forget that you get most of the money and even the whole thing back on sale!
  • It is recommended to find a caravan with solar panels or an electrical connection to the vehicle engine to charge the caravan battery
  • It is recommended to find a caravan with "self contained" certificate that allows accommodation almost anywhere in New Zealand, if it does not have this certificate it costs 500 NZD to get the certificate
  • In New Zealand you drive on the left side
  • The permitted travel speed for a vehicle towing a caravan is 90 km/h
  • No need to buy an expensive towing car 3k-6k NZD It is enough, just make sure it can tow the trailer according to its allowable towing load
  • Always take the towing vehicle for inspection before buying in a car garage, it costs 60-120 NZD
  • It is recommended to buy and sell the caravan in large city and it should be take about a week to find and buy a caravan and about two weeks to sell it
  • It is not possible to open a bank account with a tourist visa in New Zealand, you must bring the cash with you or prepare the money in the bank account for a bank transfer that takes about a week.
